Capabilities

What
we source.

Our network covers the full range of subtractive manufacturing processes, from prototype quantities to production volumes.

CNC Milling
3-axis, 4-axis, and 5-axis milling. Prismatic parts, complex contours, pockets and bores to tight tolerances.
CNC Turning
Precision turned parts, shafts, bushings, and threaded components. OD/ID to ±0.0005" on demand.
Mill-Turn / Swiss
Complex rotational parts requiring milling features. Swiss screw machining for high-volume small-diameter parts.
Secondary Operations
Anodizing, plating, heat treat, grinding, EDM, and CMM inspection — coordinated end to end.
